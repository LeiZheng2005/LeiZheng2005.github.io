Research 研究方向

▼

Research interests:

  • How can we most efficiently use non-invasive devices to collect and analyze EEG signals and upload the data to the cloud for synchronization?
  • On this basis, how to block EEG signals from controlling the human body?

Main directions:

  • Computational cognitive neuroscience: brain-like intelligence, brain-computer interface, neural network, etc.
  • Interdisciplinary fields: neuroscience, artificial intelligence, etc.

研究兴趣:

  • 如何最高效地使用非侵入式装置收集并解析脑电信号上传云端同步数据呢？
  • 在此基础上，如何阻隔脑电信号对人体的控制呢？

主要方向:

  • 计算认知神经科学: 类脑智能，脑机接口，神经网络等
  • 交叉领域: 神经科学，人工智能等
